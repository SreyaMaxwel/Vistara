Support and Counselling

Empowering women through guidance and care.

Mental Health Support

Mental health is vital for overall well-being. We provide resources and assistance to help women manage stress, anxiety, and depression.

Key Points:

  • Access to licensed counselors and therapists.
  • Stress management workshops and self-care tips.
  • Helplines for immediate support.
Infographic: Stress management techniques and mental health tips.

Career Counselling

Guiding women to explore and achieve their career goals through personalized counselling sessions and resources.

  • Resume building and interview preparation.
  • Workshops on skill development and professional growth.
  • Connecting with mentors and industry experts.

Abuse and Trauma Support

Offering a safe space for survivors of abuse and trauma to heal and rebuild their lives.

  • Confidential support groups and one-on-one sessions.
  • Legal assistance and guidance.
  • Emergency shelters and rehabilitation programs.

Community and Peer Support

Building a supportive community where women can share their experiences and uplift one another.

  • Online forums and discussion groups.
  • Community events and networking opportunities.
  • Peer mentoring programs.
